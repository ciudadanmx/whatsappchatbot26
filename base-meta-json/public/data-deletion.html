Data Deletion Request

Last updated: May 2026

Users may request deletion of personal information or conversation data associated with Publia AI Chatbot services.

How to Request Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please send an email to:

hola@publia.mx

Include sufficient information to help identify your records, such as:

  • Phone number used during conversations
  • Email address (if applicable)
  • Date or approximate timeframe of interaction

Processing Time

Requests are generally processed within 15 business days, unless additional verification or legal retention obligations apply.

Exceptions

Certain operational, legal, security, fraud prevention, or compliance-related records may be retained where necessary.

Third-Party Services

If conversations or interactions occurred through third-party businesses or integrated platforms, additional privacy policies or deletion procedures may also apply independently.
